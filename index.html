This is Refugee Tech


In todays modern society, technology is at the core of our development and new IT and technology solutions make a large impact both for refugees and for peoople interested in helping refugees. We need to connect ideas with the right competences.


Refugee tech will provide this help through an open innovation approach, reducing the effects and results from conflicts experienced while driving long-term economic development, productivity growth, venture creation and employment generation.
