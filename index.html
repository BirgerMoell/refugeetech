This is Refugee Tech


We build bridges between all parts of society to enable refugees’ potential, relying on efficiently designed and developed technology solutions.

In order to solve relevant problems that refugees face, we involve all the stakeholders with knowledge about the crises, including those who are trying to reach out to refugees as well as the refugees themselves.

Our method identifies, using methods backed by science and research, the most relevant and urgent problems that can have an innovative tech solution. The best ideas that result from our process get implemented into products that will be released and these products will improve life for thousands of people.

1. Problem identification

No single organization or person can know or understand what problems are prioriritized, and if solved, will affect the most people. This type of analyzis can only be done through a joint effort. This is what this phase is about. Using scientifically based methods for data gathering, using a structured process, we convey as many relevant stakeholders as possible to understand, prioritize and do proper impact analysis to lay the groundwork for others to come up with sustainable solutions.

2. General ideas

Using an innovative approach to hackathons we allow for the best solutions to become realized as prototypes. But this phase is much more than coding. Without proper resource commitments it would be impossible to take prototypes to developed products. Our process is designed so that promising solutions will be developed and market tested through dedicated teams, in cooperation with relevant stakeholders, with proper funding.

3. Collaborative coding

Naturally Refugee Tech's products will be open source to allow for anyone to contribute to development. Anyone, anywhere will be able to use our products and acquired knowledge to develop it further and use them in their local market. It is our mission to be a great facilitator of technology solutions. But technology is only one part of any service and service development. Value modeling, market testing or service networks are all equally important for a successful product launch. This phase is about creating the necessary foundation to release a successful product on our local market. This will lead the way for global initiatives applying similar solutions using our recipes for success(or failure).

4. Product release

In order to solve relevant problems that refugees face, we involve all the stakeholders who are trying to reach out to refugees, refuges themselves, and knowledge about the past crisis. This process identifies, using methods backed by science and research, the most relevant and urgent problems that can have an innovative tech solution. The best ideas that result from the process get to a new process of collaborative coding in order to make them a releasable product.
